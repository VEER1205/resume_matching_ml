Everything you need to know about Resume Matcher

What does this tool do?

  • Ranks resumes based on how well they align with a given job description
  • Compares skills and overall text content to produce a relative relevance score
  • Helps compare resumes—not to make hiring decisions

How does the matching work?

Skill Overlap

  • Extracts predefined technical skills from both the job description and the resume
  • Measures how many required skills are present

Text Similarity

  • Compares the overall content of the resume and job description
  • Captures alignment even when wording differs

These signals are combined into a single score used for ranking.

What does the score mean?

  • Higher score = Resume is more aligned with the job description
  • Lower score = Resume and job description discuss different skills or roles
  • The score is relative, not a percentage or probability

What this tool does NOT do

  • It does not decide whether a candidate should be hired
  • It does not judge candidate quality or seniority
  • It does not guarantee job suitability
  • Human review is always required

Interpreting the results

Use the ranking to:

  • Compare multiple resumes against the same job description
  • Identify missing or mismatched skills
  • Understand why one resume ranks higher than another

Do not use the score as a final decision.

Features of Resume Matcher

A sophisticated approach to resume-job matching

01

Resume–Job Relevance Ranking

Ranks resumes based on how closely they align with a given job description. Higher-ranked resumes are more relevant to the role requirements.

02

Hybrid Matching Approach

Combines explicit skill overlap with textual similarity to avoid the limitations of pure keyword matching.

03

Skill Match Breakdown

Clearly shows matched skills and missing skills, so you can understand why a resume ranks higher or lower.

04

Textual Similarity Analysis

Uses text similarity to capture alignment even when skills are phrased differently or mentioned indirectly.

05

Relative Scoring, Not Decisions

Provides relative relevance scores, not hiring decisions. The system ranks resumes—it does not judge candidate quality or job readiness.

06

Consistent and Reproducible Results

Uses a fixed, pre-trained text representation to ensure stable and repeatable rankings across runs.

07

Transparent and Explainable

No black-box predictions. Every score is based on understandable signals that can be inspected and explained.
